There will be no water in part of Bishkek on November 17

There will be no water in part of Bishkek on November 17. This was reported to the mayor's office. From 9 am to 8 pm, blackouts are expected on Abakanskaya, Deng Xiaoping, Kustanaiskaya, Profsoyuznaya, Volkova, Sadygaliev, and Bezymyannaya streets. The measures are related to the repair work at the water intake.
